Jorge Pina Colás

Graphics and Imaging Lab, Universidad de Zaragoza

I am a PhD student at the Graphics and Imaging Lab, working under the supervision of Prof. Belen Masia and Prof. Ana Serrano. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and my Master's degree in Robotics, Graphics and Computer Vision at Universidad de Zaragoza (Spain). I started my PhD in 2024, and my thesis focuses on the study of multisensory immersive environments in Virtual Reality through the use of physiological and behavioral signals.

Location: Zaragoza, Spain
